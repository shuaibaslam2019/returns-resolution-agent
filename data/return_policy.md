# B2B Return & Refund Policy
**Version 3.2 — Effective October 1, 2024**
**Elvinci Returns Management GmbH**

---

## 1. Overview

This policy governs all return, refund, and replacement requests from registered B2B customers. All returns must be processed through the official Returns Management System (RMS). This policy supersedes all prior versions.

---

## 2. Return Eligibility Windows

| Return Reason          | Eligibility Window       | Refund Type              |
|------------------------|--------------------------|--------------------------|
| Defective / DOA        | 12 months from delivery  | Full refund or replacement |
| Wrong item delivered   | 60 days from delivery    | Full refund + return shipping paid by us |
| Damage in transit      | 30 days from delivery    | Full refund or replacement, subject to photo evidence |
| Overstocking / no longer needed | 30 days from delivery | Store credit only (restocking fee 15%) |
| Warranty claim         | Within stated warranty period | Repair, replacement, or pro-rata refund |
| Safety / hazard issue  | Any time within warranty | Priority replacement, escalation to technical team |

---

## 3. Condition Requirements

- **Unopened / sealed**: Eligible for full refund (within policy window).
- **Opened, unused**: Eligible for store credit only; 15% restocking fee applies.
- **Used / partially deployed**: Eligible only for warranty or defective claims; not eligible for overstocking returns.
- **Damaged by customer**: Not eligible for return or refund.
- **Missing original packaging**: Accepted for defective/damage claims only; 10% deduction for missing packaging on other return types.

---

## 4. High-Value & Safety Items

Items with unit value over €5,000 (e.g., forklifts, conveyor modules, RFID infrastructure) are subject to:
- On-site inspection before return approval
- Minimum 5-business-day processing time
- Technical team sign-off required
- Safety-related defects are treated as Priority 1 and bypass standard timelines

---

## 5. Refund Methods

- **Full refund**: Credited to original payment method within 10 business days of return receipt.
- **Store credit**: Issued as a voucher code within 3 business days; valid for 24 months.
- **Replacement**: Dispatched within 5 business days of return receipt (subject to stock availability).
- **Pro-rata refund**: Calculated based on remaining warranty period.

---

## 6. Required Documentation

All return requests must include:
- Order ID
- Product SKU and quantity
- Reason for return (with supporting evidence for transit damage or defect claims)
- Photos for transit damage claims (minimum 3 photos: outer packaging, inner packaging, damaged item)
- For safety claims: error codes, incident log, or technician's report

---

## 7. Non-Returnable Items

The following are not eligible for return under any circumstances:
- Custom-configured or custom-labelled equipment
- Consumables (labels, ribbons, shrink film) once packaging is opened
- Software licenses once activated
- Items purchased under clearance or non-returnable sale

---

## 8. Return Shipping

- **Defective / wrong item / transit damage**: Return shipping costs covered by Elvinci. We issue a prepaid label within 2 business days.
- **Overstocking / change of mind**: Return shipping at customer's cost.
- **Warranty claims**: Return shipping covered by Elvinci if defect is confirmed.

---

## 9. Priority Levels

| Priority | Criteria                                                        | Response SLA    |
|----------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------|
| P1       | Safety hazard, operations fully blocked (>1,000 units/day)      | 4 business hours |
| P2       | Partial operations impact, wrong item, DOA                      | 1 business day  |
| P3       | Overstock, low-urgency warranty claims                          | 3 business days |
| P4       | Administrative returns, account credits                         | 5 business days |

---

## 10. Escalation

If a return request cannot be resolved within the stated SLA, it is automatically escalated to the Regional Account Manager. For P1 cases involving safety defects, the Technical Response Team is notified immediately.
